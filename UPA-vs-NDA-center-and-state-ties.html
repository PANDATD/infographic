Development Needs Stability

Political stability and shared priorities between the Centre and State are crucial. When synergy exists, execution is smooth. In contrast, confrontational politics can stall projects, deter investors, and create uncertainty, harming the state's progress.

From Red Zones to Growth Zones

Sustained anti-Naxal operations have nearly wiped out extremism in Maharashtra. Former red zones like Gadchiroli are now being integrated into the development mainstream with new roads, schools, and connectivity.
