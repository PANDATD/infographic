Pillars of the Creative Ecosystem

WAVES is more than a summit; it's an integrated ecosystem designed to nurture talent from education to global market access.

WAVEX

To make India a global hub for innovation in digital media and entertainment technologies by fostering a creator-first ecosystem.

  • Received 1,500 applications.
  • 30 selected startups pitched to 29 top-tier investors.
  • 127 startups successfully connected with investors.

WAVES Bazaar

To create the world's first comprehensive M&E Ecosystem, revolutionising collaboration, funding, and distribution.

  • Facilitated over ₹1,328 crore in business.
  • Hosted over 3,000 B2B meetings.
  • Registered over 5,500 buyers and 2,000 sellers.

Creatosphere

To realise the mission of "Create in India, Create for the World" by building a global creative movement.

  • Featured 32 distinct creative challenges.
  • Attracted 101,349 registrations from 60+ countries.
  • 750 finalists showcased their projects.

Global Media Dialogue

To position India as a Vishwa-Guru (global guide) in the creative domain by leading international conversations.

  • Attended by ministers from 77 nations.
  • Unanimous adoption of the 'WAVES Declaration'.
