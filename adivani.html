Expanding Language Support

Current Development Status

The app is launching with a strong foundation of four major tribal languages, with a clear roadmap for including more to broaden its reach and impact.

Available in Beta

  • Gondi: Chhattisgarh, Madhya Pradesh
  • Bhili: Madhya Pradesh, Gujarat, Maharashtra, Rajasthan
  • Mundari: Jharkhand
  • Santhali: Odisha

Coming Soon

  • Kui: Odisha
  • Garo: Meghalaya, Assam, Tripura

Synergy with National Initiatives

Adi Vaani is not a standalone app; it's a powerful tool designed to amplify the impact of key national missions, driving inclusive growth and empowerment for tribal communities.

Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat

Fosters national unity by preserving and promoting India's rich linguistic diversity.

Digital India

Integrates tribal populations into the digital economy, ensuring access to technology and services.

PM JANMAN

Delivers critical education and healthcare information to Particularly Vulnerable Tribal Groups (PVTGs) in their native tongue.

Dharti Aba Jan Jatiya...

Facilitates the effective implementation of welfare schemes and social infrastructure projects in tribal villages.

Empowering Community Leaders

Through the Adi Karmayogi Abhiyan, the Adi Vaani app will be a key tool in training village-level leaders to effectively disseminate information about government schemes.
