Trend insight

    Method note

    The uploaded state series is percentage-based. The uploaded national narrative provides the total forest area series and VDF, MDF, and OF component values. The dashboard stays explicit about that split instead of inventing values that are not present in the files.

    Insight cards

    Short, data-focused insights distilled from the uploaded Word analysis.

    National arc

    From 2001 to 2021, the uploaded analysis describes broad stability with incremental gains. The abrupt 2023 decline becomes the decisive break in the series.

    Ecological concentration

    The Northeast dominates forest density, while central India carries major forest area in absolute terms. Plains and industrial belts remain thinner in cover.

    Policy signal

    Monitoring alone does not conserve forests. The data points back to conservation quality, afforestation discipline, and land-use management.

    Data story sections

    The pattern is geographic as much as statistical: dense rainfall regions stand apart from the settlement-heavy plains.

    Period reading: 2001–2005 stayed broadly stable; 2005–2015 showed gradual growth; 2015–2021 reached a high plateau; 2021–2023 marked a sharp contraction across the national component series.
