Societal Concerns — Governance-Oriented Public Perception Analysis
This interface interprets 52 high-salience responses drawn from a broader 83-response classification exercise. The visible layer focuses on five dominant clusters: neutral evaluation, health risk, family structure, ideological concern, and mental maturity. The goal is to present the data with clarity, precision, and a governance-facing narrative grounded in social stability, public reasoning, and policy relevance.
